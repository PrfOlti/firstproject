Mother Teresa

The catholic nun who dedicated her life to caring for the destitute and dying in the slums of Calcutta.


Here's a time line of Mother Teresa's life;

  • 1910-Born as Anjezë Gonxhe Bojaxhiu un Skopje on August 26.
  • 1928-Left home at the age of 18 to join the Sister of Loreto in Rathfarnbam,Ireland.
  • 1929-Arrived in India in Darjeeling and began her novitiate.
  • 1931-May 24,took her first religious vows as a nun and chose to be named "Teresa".
  • 1950-October 7recived Vatican permission to start the Missionaries of Charity.
  • 1979-Recived the Nobel Peace prize.
  • 1996- Was operating 517missions in over 100countries.
  • 1997-March 3,stepped down from the head of Missionaries of Charity died on September5.
  • 2003-Beatified as "Blessed Teresa of Calcutta.
  • 2015- Declared "Saint" by the Roman Catholic Church.
